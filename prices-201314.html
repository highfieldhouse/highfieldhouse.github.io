To make a booking or enquiry, please phone us on 01263 825233 or email email@highfieldsheringham.com

Weekends, Bank/School Holidays and Single nights, may be subject to an additional supplement.

Please note we may require a minimum stay of 2 nights during our busy periods (or 3 nights at bank holiday weekends).

We are flexible regarding arrival time, to fit in with your plans. We ask that rooms are vacated by 10:30 am on day of departure.

We'll require a deposit equal to the cost of the first night, paid by cheque within 5 days of booking. If you have to cancel, we'll return your deposit, less 5% handling charge, as long as you give us 14 days notice prior to your booked stay, else your deposit will become non-refundable. You may want to consider travel insurance to cover this eventuality.

Single Nights.    We're more than happy for you to stay for just the one night, we know you'd stay longer if you could, though we do charge a single night supplement, we're still great value.
