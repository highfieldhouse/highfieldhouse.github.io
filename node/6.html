How to find us...

You can find us at 5 Montague Road, Sheringham, Norfolk, NR26 8LN. Parking is on-street, though it's a quiet road and you won't have any trouble getting a spot.

By road:

From the A148 take the A1082 into Sheringham. Follow the road down to the roundabout, turn left onto the A149, then next right into Church Street. At the bottom of the hill turn left just before the church into The Boulevard, and left again into South Street. Montague Road is at the end of South Street. We are situated on the left-hand side.

By rail:

From the station, walk straight ahead along Railway Approach, take the second right into St. Peter's Road, at the crossroads by the church, continue straight over along The Boulevard and take the first left into South Street. Montague Road is at the end of South Street. We are situated on the left-hand side.
